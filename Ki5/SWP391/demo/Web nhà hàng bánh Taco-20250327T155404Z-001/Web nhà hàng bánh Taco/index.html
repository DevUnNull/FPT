About Tica's Tacos

Tica's Tacos is one of the most famous taco shops in Mexico City, offering you a special culinary experience with the flavorful Tacos al Pastor. Established since the 1950s, Tica's Tacos has become an icon of Mexican street food, serving not only locals but also visitors from all over the world. We are proud to bring you great tacos made with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes, creating unforgettable flavors, typical of Mexican culinary culture.
